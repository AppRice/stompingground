How will my map be used?

The information you share on your map will help people working to make the neighborhood safer for everyone. We're not saving anything except the locations and comments you add. Make your comments descriptive so the team at Local Walking Activists can learn from them.

What's this website?

Stomping Ground is a project from OpenPlans. We want to make it easy for anyone to tell their neighbors about good and bad places to walk. We're imagining that neighbors who care about walking issues can set up a map for their neighborhood, or schools can use it to map what's good and bad about walking to school. Together, all the information added on individual maps will show a detailed picture of walking in that area.

This is the first release of Stomping Ground, so there are lots of things it doesn't yet do. Help us test out the idea of mapping good and bad walking places with this simple interface. Try it out on a desktop computer or a tablet, then tell us what you think - what's good, what's bad, what else do you want to see?
