Make a walking map...

Use this website to make a simple map of good and bad places to walk around your neighborhood!

  • Mark the places you love to walk.
  • Report problematic or dangerous places.
  • Leave comments about your walk.
  • Share your map with friends.

How will my map be used?

The information you share on your map will help people working to make the neighborhood safer for everyone. We're not saving anything except the locations and comments you add. Make your comments descriptive so the team at Local Walking Activists can learn from them.

What's this website?

Stomping Ground is a project from OpenPlans. We want to make it easy for anyone to tell their neighbors about good and bad places to walk.

Try it out on a desktop computer or a tablet, then tell us what you think - what's good, what's bad, what do you want to see?
